Expert Tips

Insurance Lene Se Pehle Yeh Zaroor Janein

⏱️
15-Day Free-Look Period
Policy milne ke 15 din ke andar terms review karo. Pasand na aaye toh cancel kar sakte ho bina penalty ke.
✅
Sahi Jaankari Do
Health history aur details hamesha sach batao — galat info claim reject kara sakti hai.
🏆
No-Claim Bonus (NCB)
Claim-free year par renewal premium par discount milta hai — yeh up to 50% tak ho sakta hai.
🔄
Samay Par Renew Karo
Policy expire hone se pehle renew karo — lapse hone par NCB aur waiting period benefits kho jaate hain.
🏥
Network Check Karo
Apne sheher ke network hospitals ya garages ki list pehle check karo — cashless claim sirf wahan milega.
📋
Waiting Period Samjho
Pre-existing diseases ke liye usually 2-4 saal ki waiting period hoti hai — policy lene mein der mat karo.
FAQ

Aksar Pooche Jaane Wale Sawaal

Minimum ₹10-15 lakh health cover family ke liye recommended hai, khaaskar metros mein. Ek ICU admission ya surgery aasaani se ₹5-7 lakh kha jaata hai. Age badhne ke saath cover bhi badhao.
50 saal tak ke logo ko adhiktar companies medical test nahi maangti. 50+ ke baad kuch basic tests ho sakte hain. Pre-existing conditions hain toh report submit karni padti hai.
Cashless claims mein real-time approval milti hai — usually 30 minutes. Reimbursement claims mein 7-15 din lagte hain agar saare documents sahi hain. IRDAI mandate hai ki 30 din ke andar settle ho.
Haan — 15-day free look period mein full refund milta hai. Baad mein cancel karne par pro-rata refund milta hai baki bache time ke liye.
Ek claim-free policy year ke baad renewal premium par discount milta hai. Health mein 25-50% tak, Motor mein 20% se 50% tak (5 saal mein).
Agar sum insured exhaust ho jaaye, restore feature ek baar phir 100% coverage wapas karta hai — same policy year mein. Niva Bupa mein unlimited times hai.
Haan — parents ko family floater mein add karne se premium bahut zyada badh jaata hai. Alag senior citizen plan zyada affordable aur better coverage deta hai.
